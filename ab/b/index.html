Production workflow

Simple, repeatable, and visible.

We keep intake, queueing, and pickup straightforward so production work keeps moving.

  1. Intake + labeling

    Clay body, glaze notes, and handling instructions stay with each batch.

  2. Queue planning

    Loads are grouped by size and firing needs to keep schedules tight.

  3. Post-fire staging

    Finished work cools, gets checked, and is staged for pickup.
