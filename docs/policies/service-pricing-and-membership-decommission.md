# Service Pricing and Membership Decommission Decision

Status: OPERATIONAL_TRUTH
Owner: policy
Decision date: 2026-04-28
Last verified: 2026-04-28

## Decision

Monsoon Fire has decommissioned all membership tiers and will use straight pricing for services only.

Kiln firing service pricing has three lanes:

- Low fire
- Mid fire
- Custom

Each kiln service lane is priced by the half shelf. Monsoon Fire does not use volume pricing, cubic-inch pricing, or membership-tier pricing for kiln services.

## Current Surface State

Website and portal content changes are intentionally paused while a separate redesign is in progress. Existing public copy, generated support inventory, or portal surfaces that mention membership tiers, member-only benefits, whole-kiln pricing, bisque/glaze pricing lanes, firing credits, storage discounts, or volume-based matching should be treated as stale until the redesign updates them.

## Agent Use

Agents may use this decision as operational context for planning and contradiction review.

Agents must not edit website, portal UI, or customer-facing pricing surfaces from this decision without explicit user approval while the redesign is active.

Pricing amounts are not set by this decision. Do not infer prices for low fire, mid fire, or custom lanes from older shelf, whole-kiln, bisque, glaze, or membership-tier copy.
