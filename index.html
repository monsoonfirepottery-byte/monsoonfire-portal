Kiln rental workflow

Your work, our kilns.

The MF Portal allows us to digitally track all work throughout our services.

  1. Intake + labeling

    Clay body, glaze notes, and handling instructions stay with each batch.

  2. Queue planning

    Work is added to the appropriate kiln queue. Whole kiln rental or expedited firings available.

  3. Post-fire staging

    Finished work cools, gets checked, and is staged for pickup.

  4. Pickup hold

    Work ready for pickup is stored for up to 4 weeks.

All notifications and updates are handled through the portal.
