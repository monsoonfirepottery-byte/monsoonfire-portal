Kiln firing

Reliable firing with clear handling.

We fire in an L&L eQ2827-3 electric production kiln with a Genesis touch screen controller, rated to cone 10. Cone fire and ramp/hold programs are available.

All kiln loading is handled by studio technicians with a focus on safe, consistent results. A reduction kiln is planned for the property.

Why this kiln: Dynamic Zone Control and quad elements keep heat even, hard ceramic element channels protect the interior, and the Genesis touch screen with the Kiln Aid app makes monitoring straightforward.
