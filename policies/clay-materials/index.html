Policy

Clay & Materials.

We fire a shared kiln schedule, so materials need to be compatible and clearly identified.

Effective Feb 1, 2026

Key points

What to expect

  • \n Approved materials\n

    Use clay bodies and glazes compatible with our high-fire schedule. Share a data sheet if you are unsure.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Outside work requirements\n

    Outside work is our primary firing client. Pieces must be bone dry, trimmed, and free of wax or dust on the foot.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Glaze compatibility\n

    No low-fire glazes, lusters, or metallics. Stable glazes only.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Test pieces welcome\n

    If you are bringing in a new body or glaze, include a test tile so we can verify behavior.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Mark the body\n

    Label clay body and firing type on each piece so it stays with the right load.

    \n
