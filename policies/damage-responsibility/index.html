Policy

Damage & Responsibility.

Ceramics are fragile. We handle work carefully and communicate fast if issues arise.

Effective Feb 1, 2026

Key points

What to expect

  • \n Built-for-firing\n

    Pieces must be structurally sound and appropriate for the firing temperature and schedule.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Normal variation\n

    Warping, pinholes, or glaze defects can occur. We do not guarantee outcomes.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Unknown materials\n

    We are not responsible for damage caused by unapproved clay or glaze materials.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n If we cause it\n

    If a piece is damaged due to handling or a kiln issue, we will review it case-by-case and make it right as best we can.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Packaging for pickup\n

    Bring padding or boxes for transport if you need extra protection.

    \n
