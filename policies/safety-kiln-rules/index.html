Policy

Safety & Kiln Rules.

Safety-first practices keep the studio breathable and the kiln room predictable.

Effective Feb 1, 2026

Key points

What to expect

  • \n Dust control always\n

    No dry sanding, dry sweeping, or compressed air. Use wet cleanup and the studio vacuum.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n PPE for dusty tasks\n

    Wear a fitted mask or respirator and eye protection for sanding or mixing dry materials.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Kiln room access\n

    The kiln room is staff-only unless invited. Do not open kiln lids or adjust controls.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Heat safety\n

    Treat shelves and posts as hot until cleared. Keep combustibles out of the kiln area.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Labeling and staging\n

    Label every piece with your full name and firing type before it enters the kiln queue.

    \n
