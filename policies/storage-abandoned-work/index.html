Policy

Storage & Abandoned Work.

Storage is limited, so we keep a clear timeline and communication.

Effective Feb 1, 2026

Key points

What to expect

  • \n Standard hold\n

    Finished work is held for 4 weeks after notification.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Two warnings\n

    We send two reminders before work is considered abandoned.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Member storage\n

    Members have access to lockers and long-term storage for active work.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Abandoned work\n

    After the hold period, unclaimed work may be recycled, donated, or discarded.

    \n
    • \n
  • \n Label everything\n

    Keep your name and date on storage bins so we can avoid mix-ups.

    \n
