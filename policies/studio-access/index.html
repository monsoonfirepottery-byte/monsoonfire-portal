Policy

Studio access & supervision.

Studio access is reservation-based so we can keep the workspace calm, clean, and safe for production work.

Effective Feb 1, 2026

Key points

What to expect

  • Reservations required

    All studio visits are scheduled through the portal to keep equipment and work areas available.

  • Check-in details

    Access instructions and any updates are shared in the portal before your visit.

  • Supervised equipment

    Some tools require staff oversight. If supervision is needed, we will note it in your reservation.

  • Shared space care

    Clean your station, return shared tools, and leave surfaces ready for the next maker.
