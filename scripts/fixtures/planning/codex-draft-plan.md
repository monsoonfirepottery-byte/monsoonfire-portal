# Planning Council Draft

## Objective
- Produce a planning-only council workflow that can review a Codex-generated plan before implementation starts.

## Why Now
- We want the first draft plan to get sharper before anyone writes code.

## Constraints
- Planning-only.
- No irreversible actions.
- Preserve clear go/no-go review before implementation.

## Steps
1. Generate the initial plan in Codex planning mode.
2. Submit the draft plan to the governed planning council.
3. Let the council review stakeholders, risks, evidence gaps, and failure paths.
4. Produce a final human arbitration packet with dissent and required decisions.
5. Only after explicit go/no-go should implementation begin.

## Validation Gates
- The final packet isolates required human decisions.
- Critical objections are either adopted or escalated.
- Rollback and recovery thinking is present where needed.

## Risks
- The initial draft plan may hide missing stakeholders.
- The council may overfit if it does not challenge the first framing.

## Required Human Decisions
- Confirm whether the reviewed plan is approved for implementation.

## Open Questions
- Should the council review happen automatically after every non-trivial `/plan`?
