Program pillars

What monthly community includes.

Events are designed for practicing makers and production-minded growth.

Game nights

Low-pressure hangouts for members and active clients to connect outside work blocks.

Ceramics process workshops

Hands-on sessions focused on clay handling, firing prep, glaze consistency, and output quality.

Business mentoring

Applied sessions for pricing, production planning, and client delivery systems.

Meet and greets

Occasional introductions for new members and local makers entering the studio network.
