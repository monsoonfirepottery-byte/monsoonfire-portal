Accessibility statement.

Monsoon Fire is committed to making our website usable for all visitors, including people with disabilities.

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Our baseline

How we approach accessibility

  • Standard

    We target WCAG 2.2 AA for the public website.

  • Keyboard + screen reader support

    Core pages are tested for keyboard-only navigation and screen-reader clarity.

  • Readable content

    We work to maintain clear headings, labels, contrast, and predictable interactions.

  • Media accessibility

    Public speech media requires captions, and long-form media requires transcript access.

    Read the media accessibility standard
