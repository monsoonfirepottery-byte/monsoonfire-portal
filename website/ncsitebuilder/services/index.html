What to expect

Clear process, no surprises.

We keep studio operations explicit so you can plan your work with confidence.

  • Booking Appointment-only access

    Reserve studio time before arrival so equipment and space are ready.

  • Firing flow Queue visibility in portal

    Track drop-off to ready-for-pickup with status updates and notes.

  • Billing Charged after service milestones

    Pricing and policy are documented before you commit work.

  • Support Direct studio follow-up

    Questions route to staff through support channels and policy-backed responses.

Studio resources

Production tools and shared resources.

Tap any item for details.

Skutt Steven Hill wheel
A steady, production-ready wheel setup for focused throwing sessions, with room to work at your pace.
Glaze kitchen
A shared mixing and testing area for recipes, small batch runs, and glaze experiments.
Sanding / painting station
A vented hood and dedicated surface area for sanding, finishing, and touch-up work.
Pugmill
De-airing and mixing for reclaim runs, keeping recycled clay consistent and usable.
Drop-off / pickup station
A secure handoff spot for kiln work when you are coming in or out of the studio.
Handbuilding tables
Open tables for slab work, texture studies, and handbuilt assembly.
Mold making
Space and tools for plaster mold work and multi-part setups.
Slipcasting
Stoneware slip access and setup for plaster mold casting.
3D printers
PLA and SLA printing for jigs, templates, and custom mold parts.
Projection demo area
A large-format projection wall for demos, references, and group viewing.
