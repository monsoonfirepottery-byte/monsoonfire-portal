Support playbook

Client workflows in plain language.

These guides cover how to start, what to expect, and how credits/billing are handled.

1. Account and intake

Set up your portal profile, then submit complete batch info in one pass.

  • Create account and verify contact email.
  • Submit clay body, glaze notes, and firing intent.
  • Use support if address or access details are missing.

2. Firing and pickup windows

Typical turnaround is about 1.5 days with pieces unloaded before pickup.

  • Most owners pick up within about 2 weeks.
  • Maximum storage window is up to 4 weeks.
  • Batch ID + photos speed up issue reviews.

3. Credits and responsibility

Credits are fault-based and reviewed at unload.

  • Studio fault: full half-shelf bisque/glaze credit.
  • Maker fault: no firing credit, leftovers released.
  • Witness cones are available on request today.

4. Billing timing

Payment timing depends on what you booked.

  • Supplies, day passes, workshops: charged immediately.
  • Workshops: charged at attendance, not reservation.
  • Firing fees: charged at unloading after review.
