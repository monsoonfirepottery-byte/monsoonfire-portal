Policy

Media accessibility standard.

Every public Monsoon Fire video or audio update must remain usable without hearing sound.

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Required before publish

Captions and transcript checklist

  • Captions are required for speech

    All spoken words in public media need accurate captions.

  • Transcript access for long-form media

    Long-form clips include a transcript link or inline transcript section.

  • No autoplay audio

    Public pages do not autoplay media with sound enabled.

  • No audio-only critical instructions

    Important updates are always available in visible text.

Need support?

Request accessible media support

If a video or audio update is missing captions or transcript support, send the page URL and media title to support.
